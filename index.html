About this Project
The Mastercraft Bamboo Moniter Riser is a sturdy and stylish platform that elevates your screen to a more comfortable viewing height. Placing the monitor at eye level has the potential to improve your posture and make your more comfortable while at work, helping you stay focused on the task at hand
Featuring artisan craftmanship, the simplicity of design creates extra desk space below your computer to allow notepads, pens and USB sticks to be stored under the stand.
